About Riverstone Center

At Riverstone Center, my purpose is to support and promote your well being. My methods include self inquiry and awareness building; empowering the client to make steps towards realizing their goals. This exploration is done through clarifying your vision, aspirations, and desires to live a more fulfilled life. It can be done in a Wellness Coaching Session, in a Core Energetics session or through Therapeutic Massage. I work with individuals, couples and groups.
